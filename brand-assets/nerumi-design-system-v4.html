Changelog

v3 → v4

Serif font replaced. Apple Garamond and Meshed Display removed. ZT Bros Oskon 90s (Zelow Type) is now Voice B — a retro-modern serif with 90s editorial character, 6 weights from Extra Light to Regular with italics. The CSS variable is renamed from --garamond to --serif.

Button shape. All buttons and badges changed from pill-shaped (border-radius: 999px) to rounded rectangles. The --radius-full token is removed entirely.

Radius tokens refined. New --radius-xs: 3px added. SM adjusted from 4px to 6px. MD adjusted from 8px to 10px. LG stays at 14px. The scale now has more visible steps.

Toggle shape. Toggle track and thumb switched from circular/pill to rounded rectangle to match the new shape language.

Serif scale specimen added. New type section showing ZT Bros Oskon 90s at display sizes (72px down to 14px) to demonstrate its editorial range.
