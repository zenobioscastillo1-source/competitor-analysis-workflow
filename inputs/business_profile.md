## What we do
Nerumi is a boutique studio offering three connected services as "one system":
**AI integrations** (LLMs made native to a business's docs, CRM, and inbox),
**workflow automation** (replacing brittle manual/Zapier-style processes), and
**website design/creation** (Editorial, Industrial, or AI-Startup styles, never
from templates). The pitch: "AI systems that still work six months later — built
by hand, run by machine." Clients can hire us for one thread and add the others.

## Target customer
Teams of **5–50** who've outgrown manual processes but aren't ready to hire a
full-time engineer — the founder copying rows between sheets at 9pm, the ops lead
whose 16-step Zapier chain breaks every Tuesday, the marketing team retyping the
same proposal in three tools. Remote-friendly worldwide.

## Positioning & differentiators
- **Reliability over demos.** Built for "the Tuesday" — the ordinary day six months
  in. Directly targets the "demo-to-production cliff" where many automations fail
  when moving from a clean test to live production.
- **You own the system.** Every engagement ships documentation, dashboards, kill
  switches, training, and credentials — a real handoff, not a zip file and goodbye.
- **Graceful failure** designed in (failover, logging) — "a broken API is a
  notification, not an outage."
- **Anti-template websites.** A considered surface so prospects stop comparing you
  on price.
- **Boutique, hand-built engagements** rather than templated delivery.
- Integrates with OpenAI, Anthropic, Notion, Slack, HubSpot, Linear, Drive,
  Calendly, Airtable.

## Pricing model
Project engagements (~30 days end-to-end) plus an optional ongoing monthly
retainer for monitoring and iteration. Pricing is by custom quote per engagement.

## Primary channels
Website with a short first call as the primary CTA ("no pitch deck, reply within
48 hours"); direct email.

## Current goals (next 1-2 quarters)
Establish a distinctive brand surface, win the "considered, reliable, design-led
automation studio" position, and convert site visitors into first calls.

## Strengths
Clear, opinionated point of view; reliability/ownership narrative; three connected
services under one roof; strong, cohesive brand and design system; anti-template
website craft.
